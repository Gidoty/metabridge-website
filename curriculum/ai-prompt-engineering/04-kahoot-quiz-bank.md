- **[RECALL]** — 3 questions (20%) — Quick fire, high energy, builds confidence
- **[CONCEPT]** — 6 questions (40%) — Core understanding check
- **[CRITICAL]** — 3 questions (20%) — Deeper thinking, often double points
- **[SCENARIO]** — 3 questions (20%) — Real-world application, Nigerian context

### KAHOOT SETTINGS RECOMMENDATIONS
- **Individual vs Team:** Team play recommended for Modules 5–8 (builds collaboration)
- **Double Points Questions:** Already marked — use for key conceptual distinctions
- **Podium Screen:** Show after Q5, Q10, Q15 to maintain engagement
- **Music:** On — maintains energy during timed questions
- **Ghost Mode:** Off for classroom sessions (competitive is more energising)
