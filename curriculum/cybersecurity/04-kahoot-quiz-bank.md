### Common Wrong-Answer Patterns to Address

| Question Topic | Common Wrong Answer | Teaching Moment |
|---|---|---|
| CIA Triad violations | Students pick "Integrity" for availability failures | Draw the three-circle diagram, label each |
| Encryption vs Hashing | Students think hashing is a type of encryption | "Meat grinder — one way. Lock — two way." |
| Authentication vs Authorisation | Students confuse the terms | AuthN = identity, AuthZ = permission |
| Symmetric vs Asymmetric | Students think asymmetric is always better | Asymmetric is slow — hybrid = best |
| IDS vs IPS | Students reverse them | IDS = watch only, IPS = watch + stop |
| Static vs Dynamic analysis | Students think dynamic is always first | Static first (safe), then dynamic (confirms) |
| CBN vs NDPC timelines | Students say 72 hours for CBN | CBN = 24 hrs (banking); NDPC = 72 hrs (data) |
| Risk formula errors | Students add instead of multiply | Risk = T × V × I (multiplication) |
