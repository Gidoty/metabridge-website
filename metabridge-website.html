Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do I need any prior experience or technical background?
▼
No prior experience is required for any of our courses. Every course is designed to start from absolute zero and take you to job-ready level. All you need is a smartphone or laptop, internet access, and the commitment to learn.
How long does each course take to complete?
▼
Every course is structured as 12 weeks, requiring approximately 6 hours per week. However, you can move faster or slower depending on your schedule. Content is available 24/7 so you learn at your own pace.
What exactly is a blockchain-verified certificate?
▼
When you complete a course, your certificate is recorded on the blockchain — a permanent, decentralised ledger. This means any employer anywhere in the world can instantly verify your credential without contacting us. It cannot be forged, altered, or lost. It exists on-chain forever.
Can I take more than one course at the same time?
▼
Yes — you can enrol in multiple courses simultaneously. Many students combine Data Analytics with Prompt Engineering, or Cybersecurity with Data Analytics for maximum career impact. Message us on WhatsApp to discuss bundle pricing.
How do I pay? Is payment in Naira available?
▼
We accept multiple payment methods including bank transfer, card payment, and cryptocurrency. Naira equivalent pricing is available. Message us on WhatsApp at 07034357206 and we will share the current Naira rates and payment details.
What happens after I enrol? How does it work?
▼
After enrolment and payment, you receive immediate access to your course materials. You are added to a WhatsApp group with your instructor and fellow students. Weekly live sessions are held, and you can ask questions at any time via WhatsApp. Labs and assignments are submitted through the provided platform.
I live outside Nigeria — can I still enrol?
▼
Absolutely. MetaBridge Academy is 100% online and open to students worldwide. We have students in the UK, USA, UAE, Canada, and across Africa. The blockchain certificate is globally recognised and verifiable from anywhere.
What support will I have during the course?
▼
You get direct WhatsApp access to your instructor, a student community group, weekly live Q&A sessions, and a dedicated support line. We believe learning should not be done alone — we are with you every step of the way.
